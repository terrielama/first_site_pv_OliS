About Me?

In 2004 I formed Bring Me the Horizon, a deathcore and metalcore band featuring several local Sheffield bands. The band released two demos in 2006, "The Bedroom Sessions" and "That Is What The Edge Of Your Seat Was Made". This was preceded by six albums: Count Your Blessings in 2006, Suicide Season in 2008, There Is a Hell and Believe Me I've Seen It. There Is a Heaven, Let's Keep It a Secret. in 2010, Sempiternal in 2013, That's the spirit in 2015, Amo in 2019, and Post Human: Survival Horror in 2020.
